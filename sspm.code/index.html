About Us

seekers offers a supportive and collaborative environment for students to learn and grow as developers

SSSPM SEEKERS. more than just seekers.

sspm seekers. is a student-led organization dedicated to fostering a community of technology enthusiasts. Our mission is to provide a platform for students to expand their knowledge, develop technical skills, and connect with like-minded individuals.

seekers. offers a variety of workshops, hackathons, and coding competitions aimed at promoting creativity, teamwork, and problem-solving skills. Members have the opportunity to work on projects that challenge their technical abilities and help them build their portfolios.

We believe in creating an inclusive and welcoming environment where students of all backgrounds and skill levels can come together and learn from each other. Whether you are a seasoned programmer or just starting out, Seekers. is the perfect place to grow your skills, meet new friends, and have fun while doing it.

  • Skill Development: Participating in the seekers. provides an opportunity to learn new programming languages, software development practices, and other technical skills.

Collaboration: Working on projects with other members of the seekers. promotes teamwork, communication, and problem-solving skills, which are all valuable in both academic and professional settings.

Why seekers.

Join the seekers to improve your coding skills, network with like-minded individuals and industry professionals, gain hands-on experience with real-world projects, access job and career opportunities, participate in fun and challenging coding competitions and hackathons, learn through collaboration, and access a library of resources. The club also provides opportunities for personal growth through leadership, communication and teamwork development. .
